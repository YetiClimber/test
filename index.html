Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

1. 2 slices sandwich bread

2. 2 tablespoons peanut butter

3. 2 tablespoons grape jelly

Directions:

1. Toast both pieces of sandwich bread in a toaster.

2. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.

3. Spread the jelly on the other side.

4. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.

5. Toddler adaptation: cut off crusts before serving.

6. Serve and enjoy!
